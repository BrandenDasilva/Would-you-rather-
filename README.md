## How It Works

1. Open `index.html` in your browser (or visit the deployed link)
2. The app presents you with a "Would You Rather" question with two options
3. Tap your preferred option
4. Submit your choice to see how everyone else voted
5. Results are displayed with percentages and vote counts
6. A new question appears automatically after viewing results (3 seconds)
7. Access question history anytime via the history button
